About Loanika

What is Loanika?
Loanika makes it easy for people in Kenya to access a loan, anytime, anywhere. Complete our application in seconds and receive your loan straight into your mobile money account. Loanika is like a bank in your pocket, there for you at all times. Visit our website to learn more.

Where is Loanika located?
Loanika has offices in Nairobi, Kenya; Lagos, Nigeria; Mexico City, Mexico; Mumbai, India; and San Francisco, United States.

In which countries does Loanika operate?
At the moment, Loanika operates in India, Kenya, Tanzania, Nigeria, and Mexico.

Do you have any vacancies?

Can I call a Loanika representative or visit the office?

My Account

How do I change my personal details?
To edit your personal details, click on this link My Account or go to “My Account” and click “Edit” under “Personal Info”.

How can I add a new mobile money number?
To register a new mobile money number, click on this link then click on “Add Account”.

How do I validate my mobile money account?
Loanika asks you to validate your mobile money account to ensure that it is a supported carrier and that it belongs to you. Click on this link , then click on the phone number labeled NOT VALIDATED that you wish to use as your Loanika phone number.

Why is my SIM unsupported?
You may see this error message if Loanika is unable to detect the required SIM in your phone. Kindly remove all other SIMs from your phone and leave only the Safaricom SIM you want to use and then log back into Loanika. You should be in a position to add your number and proceed to validate it.

Can I change my national ID number after I receive a loan?

Can I delete my account?
If you would like to delete your account, please make sure that you have repaid all of your loans and then write to us on the in-app chat with your request.

Eligibility Criteria

What is required to receive a Loanika loan?
Requirements are simple - all you need to apply is your phone number or Facebook account, National ID and mobile money account. We will also request access to the data on your phone in order determine your eligibility.

How does Loanika make a lending decision?
Loanika uses data from your phone, including your handset details and SMS messages to make lending decisions. We combine this with your Loanika repayment history to build an individualised credit score, which determines the loan offers you receive.

Why was I rejected for a loan?
If you don’t get accepted when you apply, don't worry! Sometimes it may take several attempts to qualify for a loan. We encourage you to continue saving data on your phone and to reapply after the period stipulated.

How long does the application process take?
We aim to process all loans within 24 hours. On average we process loans in less than 3 hours and hope to reduce that to a few minutes.

How can I increase my chance of being accepted?
Loanika uses data from your phone as well as other sources to make a lending decision. To increase your chances of approval, please make sure to save the data on your phone, stay current with all other lenders, and correctly input your account details.

What if someone else applied with my phone?
Once a phone has been used to access Loanika, it cannot be used by anyone else. To access Loanika, kindly use another phone that you own and that has not been used for a Loanika loan before.

Loan Offers

How do I apply for a loan?
To apply for a loan, kindly click on “My Loan”, select your repayment terms and amount, then click “Request This Loan”. Make sure that you confirm your phone number and click “Apply”.

How much can I borrow?
Our loan sizes range from Ksh 250 to Ksh 70,000. If you repay loans as scheduled, the amount that you can borrow will increase.

How do you determine my loan offers?
The loan offers you see depend on a number of factors, including your repayment history with Loanika. If you repay each installment by its due date, you may unlock access to larger loans over time. Note that the increase may not always be immediate, and that repaying your loan late may result in smaller loan offers.

Can I apply for a higher amount than shown?
Unfortunately, you won't be able to access a loan of a larger size. The fastest way to increase the amount you are eligible for is to build credit by making each repayment as scheduled.

What's the largest amount I can take?
At the moment, the highest loan amount we offer is Ksh 70,000 but this may increase in the future.

What interest rate does Loanika charge?
Interest rates are determined by a number of factors, including your repayment history with Loanika and the cost of lending for us. Depending on these factors, our rates vary as follows:

• Loan Terms: 4 Weeks - 16 Months
• Late / Rollover Fees: None
• Interest Range: 10% - 27%
• Equivalent Monthly Interest: 1% - 14%
• Equivalent APR: 12% - 180%

Borrowers are required to cover any mobile money transaction fees, such as M-PESA charges, associated with repaying the loan. Standard SMS and data charges by your mobile carrier may apply.

Will my loan size increase everytime I repay?
Your loan offers are automatically determined by a number of factors. Even though it may not always increase immediately, it will increase over time as you continue to make all of your repayments as scheduled.

How fast will my loan size increase?
Each loan amount is automatically determined by our systems each time you repay based on a number of factors. We encourage you to keep making your repayments as scheduled to increase your loan amount.

Repayment

How should I repay Loanika?
To make a repayment, follow these steps:
1. Navigate to MPesa in your Safaricom menu on your phone
2. Select Lipa na M-PESA
3. Choose the Paybill option
4. Enter the Paybill number 998608
5. For account number, enter your MPesa number on which you received your Loanika loan
6. Enter your repayment amount
7. Enter your M-PESA pin
8. Confirm that all details are correct and press ‘OK’.

Can I repay with one payment at the end of the loan term?
We advise that you make your repayments as they appear on the “My Loan” page. Skipping your other repayments and paying the loan on the last due date will negatively affect your future loan amounts.

Where can I see my repayment schedule?
To view your repayment information, kindly click on the menu on your top left and select “My Loan”. You will be able to see your installments, due dates and the amount due on those dates.

Is there a monthly repayment option?
Depending on the loan size you select, a monthly repayment option is available. Click on the blue arrow below “Select Terms” on the “My Loan” page to choose between weekly or monthly payments before requesting the loan. If you choose weekly repayments, you will need to pay each installment by its weekly due date in order for your loan to be considered on-time.

My repayment didn't reflect. What should I do?
If you do not receive confirmation from Loanika that your repayment was received within a few hours, kindly send us the transaction confirmation message you received and the number you used to make the payment via “Customer Care”.

Can I repay early?
We strongly encourage you to repay your loan on the dates available on “My Loan” to increase your loan offer amount.

What happens if I overpay my loan?
If you overpay your loan on your last repayment, the amount will remain on your account and be applied on the repayment of your next loan.

Late Repayment and CRB

What will happen if I miss my repayment?
Paying each installment by its due date allows you to access larger loan sizes. Late repayments will affect your ability to get subsequent or larger loans. If you are very late on your payments, we report to the Credit Reference Bureau (CRB). Blacklisting by the CRB may affect your ability to borrow from other lenders.

Can I request to repay my loan at a later date?
Unfortunately, Loanika cannot alter your repayment schedule after you've received a loan. If you are unable to repay the full amount due, we advise you to start making partial repayments as soon as possible.

What is a Credit Reference Bureau?
A Credit Reference Bureau (CRB) collects credit information on individuals and can provide your repayment performance data to banks, lenders, and potential employers. CRBs help promote financial inclusion and prevent over indebtedness.

What should I do when I clear my defaulted loan?
We will update and forward your details to the CRB for clearance. If you wish to acquire a certificate of clearance, kindly check with the CRB within a week of making your repayment.

To which Credit Reference Bureaus do you report?
We report to Metropol and TransUnion.

How can I get my batch number?
If your account has been cleared with the CRB, please message customer care to receive the batch numbers for Metropol and TransUnion to follow up on your certificate.

Please advise the person who invited you to Loanika to send you their promotion code, which is what you will enter in the “Promotions” page. If you were not invited by anyone or you have already repaid your first Loanika loan, the code will not be applicable. Kindly copy the code and paste it in the Promotions page.

Where can I find my invite code?
Go here. From there, you can message the person you want to invite. Your friend should then be able to click the link in the message they receive to download the app.

How will I receive my bonus?
Bonuses are automatically deducted from the amount of principal you owe on your next loan. Kindly note that your friends must enter the code on their Promotions page before fully repaying their first loan or you won’t receive your bonus.

How do I check my bonus balance?
You can check your balance here. Click on “Review your invite bonuses” to see your balance for credits received.

Why didn't I receive a credit for a friend I invited?
In order to receive the credit, kindly ensure that the person you invited registers your code on their account before they finish repaying their first loan.

Security & Privacy

Can I trust Loanika with my data?
We use world-class data security and encryption techniques to protect the data you share with us. Loanika never shares your information with third parties unless it is for dedicated business purposes, such as reporting defaulted loans to authorized credit bureaus. We do not sell your data or credit profile.

Why does Loanika need my ID?
Loanika uses your ID to ensure your account belongs to you, and so that no one else can apply for a loan using your account. We understand that this is very sensitive information and take our customer privacy very seriously. If you have additional concerns, please contact the Loanika customer service team.

Why do you need permission for my phone?
Loanika uses data from your phone, including your handset details and financial transaction messages to make lending decisions. These data are a crucial part of our decision making process, and allow us to provide seamless and efficient financial services.

How do I give permissions to Loanika on my phone?
In your Android Phone, kindly go to Settings > Apps > Loanika > Permissions and make sure that you have enabled Loanika to access everything requested.
